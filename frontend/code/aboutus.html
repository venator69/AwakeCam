Overview



AwakeCam is a health-tech startup building intelligent,non-invasive sleep detection sensors designed to improve sleep monitoring at home and in healthcare settings. Our mission is to make sleep health measurable, understandable, and actionable — without the need for wearables or intrusive equipment.


The Problem



Poor sleep is linked to a wide range of health issues, yet traditional sleep monitoring is expensive, inconvenient, and often inaccessible. Current solutions either lack accuracy or require users to wear uncomfortable devices.


Our Solution



AwakeCam uses advanced sensor technology — including motion tracking, thermal imaging, and AI-powered analysis — to passively monitor sleep patterns, detect disturbances, and provide actionable insights in real-time. The system is contactless, easy to install, and designed for both consumers and care providers.


Why Our Product?



  • Consumers: Individuals looking to improve their sleep quality and overall health.

  • Healthcare Providers: Hospitals, clinics, and sleep centers seeking non-invasive monitoring solutions.

  • Insurance Companies: Organizations interested in reducing healthcare costs through preventive care.

  • Employers: Companies aiming to enhance employee wellness and productivity.

Our Vision



  • To revolutionize sleep health by making it accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone.

  • To empower individuals and healthcare providers with the tools they need to improve sleep quality and overall health.

  • To lead the way in non-invasive sleep monitoring technology, setting new standards for accuracy and convenience.

  • To create a world where everyone can achieve better sleep and, consequently, better health.
